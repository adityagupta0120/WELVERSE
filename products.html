Trading Products Offered

Mutual Funds

Mutual funds pool money from multiple investors to invest in stocks, bonds, or other assets. Managed by professionals, they provide diversification and are a popular investment choice for long-term wealth growth.

Stocks represent ownership shares in companies. Buying stocks lets you participate in a company’s growth and earnings. Stocks can provide both capital appreciation and dividends, but involve market risk.

Futures & Options

Futures and options are derivatives giving the right or obligation to buy/sell assets at a future date. They are used for hedging risks or speculation and require knowledge of complex strategies due to their leverage.
